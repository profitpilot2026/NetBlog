# Affiliate Link Setup Guide

Here is exactly where to sign up, what the commission is, and what to replace in each article.

---

## 1. Jasper — 30% Recurring Commission

**Sign up:** jasper.ai/affiliate  
**Platform:** Impact (formerly Impact Radius)  
**Commission:** 30% recurring — you earn every month the person stays subscribed  
**Cookie window:** 30 days  

**Steps:**
1. Go to jasper.ai/affiliate and click "Become an Affiliate"
2. Fill in your details (blog URL, audience description)
3. Wait for approval — usually 1–3 business days
4. Once approved, log into Impact and go to Links → Get Link
5. Copy your unique link — it will look like: `https://jasper.ai?fpr=YOURCODE`

**Replace in articles:**  
Search all `.md` files for `YOUR_AFFILIATE_CODE` next to jasper.ai and paste your code.

## 2. Copy.ai — Recurring Commission

**Sign up:** copy.ai/affiliates  
**Platform:** PartnerStack  
**Commission:** Recurring (exact % shown after approval — typically 45 days cookie)  
**Cookie window:** 45 days  

**Steps:**
1. Go to copy.ai/affiliates
2. Apply through PartnerStack
3. Once approved, go to PartnerStack dashboard → Links
4. Your link will look like: `https://www.copy.ai/?via=YOURCODE`

**Replace in articles:**  
Search all `.md` files for `YOUR_AFFILIATE_CODE` next to copy.ai and paste your code.

## 3. Grammarly — $20 Per Premium Signup (Best CPA in This Space)

**Sign up:** grammarly.com/affiliates or via Commission Junction (CJ Affiliate)  
**Platform:** CJ Affiliate (preferred) or direct  
**Commission:** ~$20 per Premium account created (CPA — not recurring, but high per-conversion value)  
**Cookie window:** 90 days  

**Steps:**
1. Go to grammarly.com/affiliates
2. They will redirect you to CJ Affiliate — create a CJ account if you don't have one
3. Search for "Grammarly" in CJ's advertiser directory and apply
4. Once approved (can take up to a week), generate your tracking link from CJ
5. Your link will look like: `https://grammarly.com/affiliate?utm_source=...` or a CJ redirect link

**Replace in articles:**  
Article 05 and 06 have `https://grammarly.com/affiliate` as a placeholder — replace with your actual CJ tracking link.

## What to Do Before You Apply

Affiliate programs want to see a real site with real content. Before applying:
- Make sure at least 2 posts are published (you already have this ✅)
- Your blog should be on a custom domain, not a subdomain
- Have a clear niche (AI writing tools / content creation — you have this ✅)

Apply for Jasper first — it has the best recurring commission and fastest approval. Then Copy.ai, then Grammarly (CJ takes longest), then Writesonic.
