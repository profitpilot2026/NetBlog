Editorial Policy

How we decide what to cover, how commercial relationships work, and what we do when we get something wrong.

Profit Pilot is an independent publication. Our editorial decisions are not for sale. Here is how we operate.

What we cover

Profit Pilot focuses on AI tools, comparisons, and practical workflows for marketers and creators. We cover:

  • AI writing, productivity, and research tools used in marketing and content work
  • Head-to-head tool comparisons with clear use-case verdicts
  • Practical guides for building AI-assisted workflows
  • Pricing analysis and "worth paying for?" assessments

We do not cover:

  • Pure AI research papers or academic benchmarks
  • Enterprise software with no clear self-serve pricing
  • Tools we have not personally tested
  • Speculative opinion pieces about AI and society

Affiliate relationships

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and sign up for a paid plan, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate links do not affect our editorial verdicts.

Tools are reviewed using the same testing framework regardless of whether an affiliate program exists. A tool with a high-paying affiliate program that performs poorly in testing will receive a poor review. A tool with no affiliate program that performs well will be recommended. The commission structure is invisible to us when we write reviews.

Every affiliate relationship is disclosed.

Any article containing affiliate links includes a disclosure notice at the top. We follow FTC guidelines and disclose commercial relationships clearly and prominently — not buried in a footnote.

We do not accept payment for positive reviews.

Tool companies cannot pay for a review, a favourable score, a specific recommendation, or prioritised placement. Sponsored content, if ever published, will be clearly labelled as such and kept entirely separate from editorial content.

Accuracy and updates

AI tools change quickly. Pricing changes, features are added or removed, and models improve. We maintain our reviews on this basis:

Reviews are dated and updated.

Every review shows both a publish date and a last-updated date. When a tool changes materially — pricing, features, quality — we revisit the review and note what changed. We do not silently edit articles without acknowledging the revision.

Corrections policy.

If you find a factual error in any article, contact us. We take corrections seriously. If the error is confirmed, we correct it promptly and note the correction in the article. We do not delete content to avoid accountability.

Independence

Profit Pilot has no investors, no parent company, and no editorial board. Content decisions are made entirely by the editorial team. No external party influences what we publish, how we score tools, or what we recommend.

If this ever changes — for example, if we take on investment, a sponsorship that influences coverage, or any other arrangement that affects editorial independence — we will disclose it prominently.

Contact

If you have a correction, a question about our editorial standards, or want to flag an issue with a review, reach out via the About page. We read every message and respond to substantive ones.

// The short version

We only recommend tools we would genuinely use. We disclose every commercial relationship. We update content when things change. And we correct mistakes when we make them.
