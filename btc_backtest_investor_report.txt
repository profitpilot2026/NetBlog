STRATEGY OVERVIEW
─────────────────
Two strategies running in parallel on BTCUSDT 5-minute bars:

  S5 — MACD Zero-Cross Momentum
  Entry  : MACD histogram crosses above/below zero
  Filter : Price above/below EMA50, 3-bar momentum >0.1%, volume Z-score >0.5
  Trend  : 15-min EMA50 direction must agree

  S1 — Liquidity Sweep Reversal (Stop Hunt)
  Entry  : Bar wicks below 10-bar low but closes back above (false breakdown)
  Filter : Lower wick >35% of range, volume Z-score >1.5, RSI <60
  Trend  : 15-min EMA50 direction must agree

RISK PARAMETERS
───────────────
  Stop Loss   : 5.0 × ATR(14)
  Take Profit : 12.5 × ATR(14)   R:R = 2.5:1
  Risk/Trade  : 1% of equity
  Slippage    : 0.04% per side (Binance taker fee)
  Sessions    : 06:00–21:00 UTC (London + NY overlap)
  Min ADX     : 15 (skip sideways markets)
  Min Vol Ratio: 1.2× (skip low-volume bars)
  Break-even WR: 28.6%

  SHARPE RATIO INTERPRETATION
  ─────────────────────────────
  Sharpe >1.0  = Good (most hedge funds: 1.0–2.0)
  Sharpe >2.0  = Excellent
  Sharpe >5.0  = Exceptional / suspect curve-fitting
  Sharpe 15.16 = EXTREMELY high — warrants scrutiny

  A Sharpe of 15.16 is almost never seen in live trading.
  Typical causes: lookahead bias, survivorship bias,
  overfitting to one market regime, or insufficient slippage.

  PROFIT FACTOR
  ──────────────
  S5: 1.13x — For every $1 lost, $1.13 is won (marginal)
  S1: 1.11x — For every $1 lost, $1.11 is won (marginal)
  Robust strategies typically show PF >1.5. These are thin.

  WIN RATE vs EXPECTANCY
  ───────────────────────
  Win rate 32.6% with R:R 2.5:1:
  Expectancy = (0.326 × avg_win) + (0.674 × avg_loss)
  With avg_win ≈ 2.48%, avg_loss ≈ -1.02%:
  Expectancy ≈ +0.12% per trade = POSITIVE (good)

  But if win rate drops to 28% in live trading:
  Expectancy = (0.28 × 2.48) + (0.72 × -1.02) = -0.04% per trade
  This tips the system into LOSING territory.

  SECTION 4: RISK FLAGS & RED FLAGS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  🔴 RED FLAG 1 — Sharpe too high (15.16)
  Real live Sharpe will likely be 1–3x after real-market frictions.
  A Sharpe >10 on intraday crypto almost always indicates:
  - The backtest period was a bull run (Apr 2023–Apr 2024 BTC +130%)
  - The strategy is regime-dependent
  - Slippage/fees are underestimated

  🔴 RED FLAG 2 — Fails on synthetic data
  Win rates drop below break-even on different price paths.
  This strongly suggests the parameters are tuned to one
  specific market regime. This is curve-fitting.

  🟡 WARNING 3 — Thin profit factor (1.11–1.13x)
  Only 11–13 cents of profit per dollar of loss.
  One bad month can erase several good months.

  🟡 WARNING 4 — 18% max drawdown
  At 1% risk/trade on $10,000 account = -$1,800 drawdown.
  Psychologically challenging. Many traders abandon systems here.

  🟡 WARNING 5 — No walk-forward test
  The strategy was optimised (ATR_SL=5, RR=2.5) likely using the
  same data it was tested on. Need out-of-sample validation.

  🟡 WARNING 6 — Bull market only
  BTC went from ~$28K to ~$65K during the backtest period.
  Short signals would have faced strong headwinds.
  Short win rate needs separate verification.

  ✅ GREEN FLAG 1 — Positive expectancy confirmed
  32.6% WR with 2.5 R:R is mathematically sound.

  ✅ GREEN FLAG 2 — Sufficient trade count
  181+149 = 330 trades is a meaningful sample size.

  ✅ GREEN FLAG 3 — Sensible risk management
  1% risk/trade, session filter, ADX filter all show
  thoughtful design.

  ✅ GREEN FLAG 4 — Trend filter helps
  Comparing with/without trend filter: trend filter
  significantly reduces trades and improves selectivity.
