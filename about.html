// About this publication

We help marketers and creators use AI without the noise.

Profit Pilot is a practical AI publication. We test tools, compare them honestly, and help you figure out what's actually worth your time — and what isn't.

There are two problems with most AI tool content right now. Either it's a directory that lists ten thousand tools with no editorial judgment, or it's hype-driven content that calls everything "a game changer." Neither is useful when you're trying to decide whether to pay $20/month for a tool.

Profit Pilot exists to do something more useful: test AI tools with real marketing and content tasks, compare them honestly, and tell you clearly which ones are worth it for your specific workflow — and which ones you should skip.

What we believe

  • Testing before publishing. No review goes live without someone actually using the tool on real tasks.
  • Honest "who should skip it" sections. Every review includes this. It's the most useful thing we can tell you.
  • Affiliate disclosure, always. Some links earn us a commission. We say so clearly and it does not change our verdicts.
  • Updates when things change. AI tools change fast. We revisit reviews when pricing or quality shifts materially.
  • No "ultimate guide" inflation. We write what's actually useful, not what sounds impressive.
